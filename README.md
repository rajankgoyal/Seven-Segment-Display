Seven Segment Display driven by Raspberry Pico Pi W
---
In this project, I will be utilizing a Raspberry Pico Pi W along with 2 Seven Segment Displays to produce a number from 1-99.

Seven-Segament Display is created to drive two seven segment displays connected to Raspberry Pico W. Both displays combined can display numbers from -99 to 99. The negitive sign can be indicated either by turning on decimal point on right display or a LED placed infront of left display. 
Left display is connected to Pico's 0-7 GPIO pins and Right display is connected to GPIO pins 16-27
